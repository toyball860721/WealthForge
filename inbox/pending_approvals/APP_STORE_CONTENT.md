Milestone helps you track and celebrate life's important moments. Create beautiful countdowns for birthdays, anniversaries, vacations, project deadlines, and more.

FEATURES:

• Beautiful Countdowns
Create unlimited countdown events with custom icons and themes. See exactly how many days remain until your important dates.

• Custom Icons & Themes
Choose from a variety of icons to personalize each event. Make your countdowns uniquely yours.

• Smart Notifications
Get reminded about upcoming events so you never miss an important moment.

• Home Screen Widgets
See your countdowns at a glance with beautiful home screen widgets.

• iCloud Sync
Keep your events synced across all your devices with iCloud.

• Privacy First
All your data stays on your device. We don't collect or share any personal information.

• Dark Mode
Fully supports Dark Mode for comfortable viewing any time of day.

PERFECT FOR:
• Birthdays and anniversaries
• Vacation countdowns
• Project deadlines
• Special events
• Holidays
• And any important date you want to remember!

Milestone is designed with simplicity and elegance in mind. No complicated features, just beautiful countdowns that help you celebrate what matters most.

Download Milestone today and never miss another important moment!

## 🖼️ 截图要求

### 必需尺寸
1. **6.7" Display (iPhone 17 Pro Max)**
   - 分辨率: 1290 x 2796 pixels
   - 至少 3 张截图

2. **6.5" Display (iPhone 14 Pro Max)**
   - 分辨率: 1284 x 2778 pixels
   - 至少 3 张截图

### 建议截图内容
1. **主界面** - 显示事件列表，包含 3-4 个不同的倒计时
2. **事件详情** - 显示单个事件的详细信息和大号倒计时
3. **编辑界面** - 显示图标选择器和日期选择器
4. **设置页面**（可选）- 显示订阅选项
5. **小组件**（可选）- 显示主屏幕小组件

## 💡 提示

1. **IAP 配置**: 所有 IAP 产品必须在提交审核前配置完成并处于"准备提交"状态
2. **截图**: 可以使用 Xcode 模拟器截图（Cmd + S），然后用设计工具添加标题
3. **测试**: 使用沙盒测试账号测试所有 IAP 功能
