Privacy Policy for Milestone

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

TL;DR: Milestone does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. All your countdown events are stored locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information.

Milestone is designed with privacy in mind. All your data, including:

  • Event titles and dates
  • Custom icons and themes
  • Notification preferences
  • Any other app settings

...is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this information.

2. iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable iCloud sync (Premium feature), your event data will be stored in your personal iCloud account. This data:

  • Is encrypted by Apple
  • Is only accessible by you
  • Is never accessible by us
  • Follows Apple's iCloud privacy policy

3. Notifications

Milestone uses local notifications to remind you about upcoming events. These notifications:

  • Are generated locally on your device
  • Do not send any data to external servers
  • Require your permission to function

4. In-App Purchases

If you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions, which may collect:

  • Anonymous purchase receipts
  • Device identifiers (for subscription validation only)

No personally identifiable information is shared with us or RevenueCat.

5. Analytics

We do not use any analytics or tracking services.

We do not track:

  • How you use the app
  • Which features you access
  • Your location
  • Any other behavioral data

6. Third-Party Services

Milestone uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For managing in-app purchases and subscriptions (anonymous data only)
  • Apple iCloud: For optional data sync (only if you enable it)

7. Children's Privacy

Milestone does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

8. Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on:

  • Your device's security settings (passcode, Face ID, etc.)
  • Apple's iOS security features
  • Your iCloud account security (if sync is enabled)

9. Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing for us to delete, modify, or export. You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Disable iCloud sync to stop cloud storage
  • Manage your iCloud data through iOS Settings

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
